                             Program SHIFTVAL
                                6 Aug 2019

shiftval <highw> <loww> <shift>

Show the value of a 32 bit fixed point number.

HIGHW is the value of the high 16 bits as a unsigned integer, and LOWW is
the value of the lo 16 bit words as a unsigned integer.

SHIFT is the number of fraction bits in the 32 bit fixed point number.
SHIFT may be negative to indicate that the binary point is actually to the
right of the number.
